Why φ?

Nature's most dramatic proportion

The golden ratio (φ ≈ 1.618) appears everywhere from spiral galaxies to sunflower seed arrangements. Applied to type, it produces the steepest natural curve — small steps stay close, but large steps become truly monumental. This is not a scale for the faint of heart.
