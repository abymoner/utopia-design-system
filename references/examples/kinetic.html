Why two ratios?

The same scale bends at every width

A fixed ratio keeps the same proportions everywhere. A dynamic ratio interpolates between two different scales — giving you a gentle hierarchy on mobile and a bold editorial voice on desktop. The typeface bends to the medium.

WCAG violation detected at step 5
The dynamic ratio produces such dramatic growth at the upper end that step 5 fails WCAG 1.4.4 between 1243 px and 2268 px viewport width. This is expected for aggressive editorial scales — the violation only affects the largest heading and can be mitigated with a separate font-size at extreme widths.
