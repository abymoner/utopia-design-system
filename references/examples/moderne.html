Why 4:3?

The most consonant interval

The perfect fourth (4:3 ratio) is one of the most fundamental intervals in music — found in the first notes of Here Comes the Bride and the overtone series of every sound. Applied to type, it creates hierarchy that feels inevitable, never forced.

Flat ratio, consistent feel

Unlike dynamic ratios, a fixed 1.333 means the proportional relationship between heading and body is identical at every viewport. What looks like a headline on mobile feels like a headline on desktop.
